KingSammelot is an American player, streamer, and content creator in Geometry Dash. He is a multi-game streamer and content creator, streaming games such as Minecraft and Fortnite, much of his YouTube content consists of Geometry Dash videos, stream highlights, and music. He has been playing the game since 2014, but he only started making Geometry Dash content in 2021 after completing Bloodbath; he has since amassed over 1,000,000 subscribers. In 2023, he was awarded the Hidden Gem Award at The Streamer Awards for the recognition of being a streamer with fewer than 100 average viewers per stream.
